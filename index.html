Picture Story

New Farming Methods

Sashaank was 21 years old and studying at agricultural college, Tamil Nadu. During his semester holidays, he wanted to visit his native village and was looking forward to it. When he went to his village, he freshened up, ate his dinner, and decided to visit the farmland in the morning. The next day morning, he woke up and went to the farmland. There he found out that the farmers were using the primitive method of agriculture. Seeing this he wanted to help them out.

With the help of the village panchayath head, he called up the village farmers for the meeting. He discussed about the method they were using for agriculture. He told them and guided them about the new farming techniques like polyculture, HVY seeds, Biodynamics, natural fertilizers, and modern machinery. Not only this, but he also told them about the government schemes like National Food Security Mission, Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, and many more.

He also introduced some new methods which increase the productivity of the crops. After the meeting, all the village farmers were happy and decided to raise sashaank’s work.
